Our Clergy

"I will instruct you and teach you in the way you should go; I will counsel you with my eye upon you." Psalm 32:8

Fr. Mina Essak, previously Mr. Kamal Essak, is a graduate of Assuit Univeristy. He graduated from the College of Engineering in 1967 with a BA in Mechanical Engineering. Kamal worked as a designer and then as a mechanical engineer with a variety of consulting firms. He joined the US federal government in 1985 working for the small business administration and then for the US Army until October of 1991. In November 1991, Kamal was ordained as Fr. Mina to serve the congregation of St. Mark Coptic Orthodox Church in Troy, MI. He was elevated to the rank of Archpriest in 1999. May the Lord continue his service.

Very Reverend Fr. Mina Essak

Fr. Maximus Habib, formerly Mr. Magdy Habib, is a graduate of Ain Shams University in Cairo, Egypt. He holds a BA in Civil Engineering which he earned in 1982. Magdy graduated from Wayne State University in 1995 with a MS in Civil Engineering. He worked as a Project Engineer with Smith Group in Detroit, MI until 2001. In July 2001, Magdy was ordained as Fr. Maximus to serve the congregation of St. Mark Coptic Orthodox Church in Troy, MI. May the Lord continue his service.

Reverend Fr. Maximus Habib

Fr. Mark Ibrahim, previously Mr. Sherif Ibrahim graduated from Wayne State University in 1998 with a BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering. He graduated from Wayne State again in 2004 with a MS in Computer Engineering and earned his MBA from Walsh College in 2008. Sherif worked as a Network Security Consultant in various fortune 500 firms until 2012. In July 2012, Sherif was ordained as Fr. Mark to serve the congregation of St. Mark Coptic Orthodox Church in Troy, MI. Mat the Lord continue his service.

Reverend Fr. Mark Ibrahim
